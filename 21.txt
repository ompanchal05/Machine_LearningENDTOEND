COMPANY-WISE INTERVIEW FOCUS (DATA / ML ROLES)
=============================================

PURPOSE
-------
This document helps you:
- Understand what different companies test
- Avoid over-preparing wrong topics
- Focus your effort smartly
- Crack interviews role-wise

Language: Simple English
Focus: Practical + realistic
Rule: Every company tests DIFFERENT things

=================================================
SECTION 1: STARTUPS (EARLY / MID STAGE)
=================================================

What startups look for:
-----------------------
- Ability to work independently
- End-to-end ownership
- Practical problem solving

Technical focus:
----------------
- Python basics
- Pandas & NumPy
- SQL
- End-to-end ML projects
- Deployment basics (Streamlit / API)

Interview style:
----------------
- Explain your project deeply
- Write small code snippets
- Real-world data problems

How to prepare:
---------------
- Know every line of your project
- Be ready to debug
- Focus on practical ML, not theory

Golden line:
------------
"I can take a problem from data to deployment."

=================================================
SECTION 2: SERVICE-BASED COMPANIES
(TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture)
=================================================

What they look for:
-------------------
- Strong fundamentals
- Clear communication
- Willingness to learn

Technical focus:
----------------
- Python basics
- SQL
- Statistics
- ML fundamentals
- Simple projects

Interview style:
----------------
- Conceptual questions
- HR + behavioral heavy
- Scenario-based questions

How to prepare:
---------------
- Revise basics daily
- Practice explaining concepts simply
- Prepare HR answers well

Golden line:
------------
"I have strong fundamentals and a learning mindset."

=================================================
SECTION 3: PRODUCT-BASED COMPANIES
(FAANG, Product Unicorns)
=================================================

What they look for:
-------------------
- Strong problem-solving
- Deep understanding
- Scalable thinking

Technical focus:
----------------
- Data structures (basic)
- ML algorithms (deep)
- Statistics
- System design (ML)
- SQL + Python

Interview style:
----------------
- Why-based questions
- Trade-offs discussion
- System design rounds

How to prepare:
---------------
- Understand why algorithms work
- Practice system design
- Explain trade-offs clearly

Golden line:
------------
"I design scalable ML systems, not just models."

=================================================
SECTION 4: DATA-FOCUSED COMPANIES
(Analytics firms, Consulting)
=================================================

What they look for:
-------------------
- Business understanding
- Data storytelling
- SQL strength

Technical focus:
----------------
- SQL (very strong)
- Statistics
- Visualization
- Case studies

Interview style:
----------------
- Case-based questions
- Data interpretation
- Business decisions

How to prepare:
---------------
- Practice SQL daily
- Learn how to explain insights
- Focus on business impact

Golden line:
------------
"I convert data into business decisions."

=================================================
SECTION 5: ML ENGINEERING ROLES
=================================================

What they look for:
-------------------
- ML + engineering skills
- Deployment knowledge
- Monitoring & pipelines

Technical focus:
----------------
- ML algorithms
- Pipelines
- MLOps basics
- APIs & deployment
- System design

Interview style:
----------------
- End-to-end ML system questions
- Debugging
- Production scenarios

How to prepare:
---------------
- Practice deployment
- Understand monitoring
- Think in systems

Golden line:
------------
"I build ML systems that work in production."

=================================================
SECTION 6: INTERNSHIP INTERVIEWS
=================================================

What they look for:
-------------------
- Fundamentals
- Curiosity
- Learning attitude

Technical focus:
----------------
- Basics of Python, SQL
- 1–2 good projects
- Clear explanations

Interview style:
----------------
- Simple questions
- Project discussion
- HR round

How to prepare:
---------------
- Revise basics
- Explain projects clearly
- Be honest

Golden line:
------------
"I may be a fresher, but I learn fast and take ownership."

=================================================
SECTION 7: COMMON MISTAKES (AVOID)
=================================================

- Preparing everything for every company
- Ignoring HR rounds
- Over-complicating answers
- Not knowing your own project

=================================================
SECTION 8: FINAL STRATEGY
=================================================

1. Identify company type
2. Adjust preparation
3. Revise relevant topics
4. Practice speaking answers
5. Stay calm and confident

=================================================
FINAL ONE-LINER REVISION
=================================================

- Startup → Practical
- Service → Fundamentals
- Product → Depth
- Analytics → Business
- ML Engineer → Systems
