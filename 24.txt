A key-value data structure.

Q4. Why use NumPy?
A:
For fast numerical computation.

Q5. What is a DataFrame?

A table-like structure for data.

-------------------------------------------------

=================================================

Q8. What is mean, median, mode?
A:
Mean → average
Median → middle
Mode → frequent

-------------------------------------------------

Q9. What is standard deviation?
A:
It shows how much data varies.

SECTION 4: MACHINE LEARNING BASICS
=================================================

Q10. What is Machine Learning?
A:
Teaching machines to learn from data.

-------------------------------------------------

Q11. Types of ML?
A:
Supervised, Unsupervised, Reinforcement.

-------------------------------------------------

Q12. What is train–test split?
A:
Splitting data into training and testing sets.

-------------------------------------------------

Q13. Why train–test split is needed?
A:
To test model on unseen data.
