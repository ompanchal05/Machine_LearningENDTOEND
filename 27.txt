INTERNSHIP & FRESHER RESUME TEMPLATE (DATA / ML ROLES)
=====================================================

PURPOSE
-------
This resume template is designed for:
- Data Analyst internships
- Data Scientist internships
- ML Engineer internships
- Freshers with projects (no experience)

Rule:
-----
Clarity > Fancy words  
Projects > Certificates > CGPA

=================================================
HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE
=================================================

- Keep resume to 1 page
- Use bullet points
- Use simple English
- Only write what you KNOW
